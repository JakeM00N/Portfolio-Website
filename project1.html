Saint Joseph Cathedral Podcast – Intro & Trivia

This project involved creating an engaging intro sequence with synchronized visuals, motion graphics, and background music for the Saint Joseph Cathedral podcast. The goal was to capture attention while highlighting the key elements of the podcast.

Project Overview

The Saint Joseph Cathedral Podcast wanted to refresh their original intro, which had become outdated. The objective of this redesign was to create a modern, engaging introduction that captures the spirit of the podcast while maintaining brand consistency. This included updating visual styles, enhancing motion graphics, and integrating seamless audio synchronization.

The project focused on delivering a professional and polished experience for both new and returning listeners, ensuring the intro not only entertains but also reinforces the podcast's identity.
