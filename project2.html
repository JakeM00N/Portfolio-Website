FSUU Computer Studies Program – Promo Video

Produced a promotional video highlighting the program’s facilities, activities, and student experiences.

Project Overview

This promo video was created for a competitive contest showcasing the FSUU Computer Studies Program. It highlights the program’s facilities, activities, and student experiences, and was designed to be both engaging and professional. The video earned first place in the competition, demonstrating its effectiveness in presenting the program in a compelling way. The university also used my video for promoting the program to prospective students and the wider community.
